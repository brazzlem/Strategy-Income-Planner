METHODOLOGY & DISCLOSURES

Data sources, calculation assumptions, and regulatory disclosures for this planning tool.
▸ Data Sources
Dividend rates sourced from Strategy Inc. SEC filings and public investor relations disclosures. Share prices reflect current market data entered by the user. Bitcoin price data is user-selected for illustrative purposes only. Coverage threshold derived from Strategy Inc.'s publicly filed financial statements.
▸ Calculation Methodology
Projections assume: (1) constant dividend rate equal to current rate at time of use; (2) full DRIP reinvestment executed at current share price each period; (3) DCA contributions made at consistent intervals with no transaction costs, taxes, or fees applied; (4) share price remains constant throughout projection. Actual results will differ materially from projections.
▸ Key Assumptions
• Dividend rate: Current declared rate — subject to monthly adjustment at Strategy's sole discretion and may be reduced or suspended
• DRIP: Assumes full reinvestment at par/market price with no fractional share limitations
• No taxes: Federal, state, or local tax treatment not modeled
• No fees: Brokerage commissions, account fees, or spreads not included
• Constant share price: No capital appreciation or depreciation modeled
