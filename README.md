# Strategy Income Planner

> Free DCA + DRIP income calculator for STRC and the full Strategy preferred suite. Goal Target mode, Bitcoin coverage scenarios, PDF export. Zero dependencies.

![Strategy Income Planner](screenshot.png)

---

## What It Does

A free, single-file income planning tool built for retail investors holding Strategy Inc. preferred stock. No signup. No backend. No ongoing cost. Just open it and run your numbers.

**Two planning modes:**

- **BUILD INCOME** — Enter what you plan to invest and how much per month. See your projected monthly income, total shares, and portfolio value over any timeline.
- **GOAL TARGET** — Enter a monthly income target. Get the exact month you reach it, how much you need to invest, and a visual crossover marker on the chart.

---

## Features

- Full Strategy preferred suite: STRC, STRF, STRD, STRK — one click to switch
- DCA + DRIP compounding modeled month by month
- Weekly or monthly contribution frequency
- Projection period from 1 month to 100 years
- Bitcoin coverage strength scenarios with accordion for extended price ranges
- 4 primary KPI cards with expandable advanced metrics
- SVG growth chart with Goal Target crossover marker
- Milestone banner injected at exact goal month in breakdown table
- PDF export with timestamped assumptions and full methodology disclosure
- Methodology and Disclosures modal with forward-looking statement disclaimer
- Strategy IR outreach email pre-written and copy-ready
- Fully responsive and mobile-friendly
- Zero external dependencies — one HTML file, works completely offline

## How To Use

1. Open the tool in any browser
2. Select your preferred instrument (STRC, STRF, STRD, or STRK)
3. Choose your planning mode — Build Income or Goal Target
4. Enter your starting investment, DCA amount, and projection period
5. Results update instantly — no button to click

**Live version:** [brazzlem.github.io/strategy-income-planner](https://brazzlem.github.io/strategy-income-planner/)

## Self Hosting

This is a single HTML file with zero external dependencies beyond Google Fonts.

**Option 1 — GitHub Pages (recommended):**
Download `index.html`, upload to any GitHub repository, enable Pages under Settings. Live in minutes.

**Option 2 — Direct embed on any website:**
```html
<iframe
  src="https://brazzlematt.github.io/strategy-income-planner"
  width="100%"
  height="900px"
  frameborder="0"
  style="border-radius:12px">
</iframe>
```

**Option 3 — Local / offline:**
Download `index.html` and open it directly in any browser. Works completely offline with no internet connection required.
